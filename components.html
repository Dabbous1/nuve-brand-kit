05.E — Feedback & Overlays
Feedback Components
Alerts, toasts, bottom sheets, loading states, and empty states — the full vocabulary of feedback for a financial app that must communicate precisely.
Alert Banners
Inline banners for success, warning, error, and informational messages. Appear within screen content, not as overlays.
Investment successful
EGP 50,000 has been invested in EGX 30 Index Fund. Settlement in T+2 business days.
Rebalancing recommended
Your equity allocation has drifted to 68%, above your 60% target. Consider rebalancing.
Transaction failed
Insufficient funds. Your available cash balance is EGP 12,400.
Market closed
EGX is closed today for a public holiday. Orders will execute on the next trading day.
Toast Notifications
Brief, dismissable overlay notifications. Appear at the top or bottom of the screen and auto-dismiss after 3 seconds.
Order placed successfully VIEW
Price alert: EGX 30 reached 25,000 DISMISS
Session expiring in 2 minutes EXTEND

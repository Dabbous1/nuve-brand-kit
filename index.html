06 — Brand Voice
Tone & Language
Nuvé speaks with quiet confidence — knowledgeable but never condescending, premium but never exclusionary. We simplify complexity without dumbing it down.
✓ Do
"Your portfolio grew 12.4% this quarter — here's what drove the gains."
✕ Don't
"Congrats! 🎉 Your money went UP! Check out these awesome returns!!!"
✓ Do
"Market conditions suggest rebalancing — we've prepared options for you."
✕ Don't
"Warning! Volatility detected! Act now before it's too late!"

07 — Imagery & Gradients
Visual Language
Photography and illustrations should feel premium, editorial, and intentional. Favor abstract financial imagery, clean data visualizations, and lifestyle shots that convey aspiration without excess.
Photography Direction
Do: Clean compositions, natural light, muted warm tones, urban professional settings, abstract data visuals, Egyptian/Middle Eastern architectural details
Don't: Stock-feeling handshakes, overly staged "business" shots, cluttered compositions, neon/saturated filters, generic global city skylines
